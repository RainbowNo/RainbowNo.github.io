GujMath Account Deletion Request

If you would like to delete your GujMath account and associated data, please send an email to:

colorlessrainbowwww@gmail.com

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email with the subject line: Account Deletion Request.
  2. Include the Google account email address used to sign in to GujMath.
  3. We may contact you to verify ownership of the account before deletion.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User profile information (name, email address, profile image URL)
  • Leaderboard entries and game scores
  • Account-related data stored for app functionality

Data Retention

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days. Certain records may be retained for a limited period if required for:

  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Legal obligations
  • Technical troubleshooting

Contact

If you have any questions regarding your account or personal data, please contact:
