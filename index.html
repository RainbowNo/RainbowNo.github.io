MATCH THE DOT

A sleek, minimalist grid-puzzle where you flood adjacent cells to match each tile’s target color.

✨ Levels • ⚡ Blitz Mode • 📅 Daily Puzzle

Elegant Core

Tap a starting cell, paint adjacent tiles (up/down/left/right), and match the tiny dot’s target color on each cell.

Progression

Multiple difficulties (Easy → Expert), smart unlocks, and a clean, satisfying completion flow.

Premium Polish

Fluid animations, glossy gradients, crisp typography, and responsive layouts that feel great.

🎯 Levels (Campaign)

Tackle handcrafted levels grouped by grid size and difficulty. Unlock new stages by clearing previous ones—or watch a rewarded ad to skip ahead if you’re stuck.

⚡ Blitz Mode

Race against the clock (1/3/5 mins). Levels get tougher as your score climbs. Beat your high score!

📅 Daily Puzzle

A fresh puzzle every UTC day. Limited attempts, shared seed, fair challenge. Come back tomorrow for more.

🧩 How to Play

  • The goal is to assign the target color to each cell.
  • The large square shows the current color; the tiny dot shows the target color.
  • Paint spreads to the four adjacent cells: up, down, left, right.
  • Complete all targets to clear the level.
  • Undo and reset are available if you need a fresh start.
  • Enjoy the flow. That’s it 🙂
